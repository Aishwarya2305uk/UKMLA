<h2>Who Welcome to UK Practice Is For</h2>
<p>The workshop is targeted deliberately at a specific stage of an IMG's journey, and understanding exactly who it is designed for helps explain why it is worth prioritising rather than treating as optional background noise.</p>

<h3>Doctors Who Have Just Registered With the GMC</h3>
<p>The most common attendee is a doctor who has recently completed the full <a href="/news#gmc-registration-requirements-for-international-doctors">GMC registration requirements for international doctors</a> — eligibility, primary source verification, English language evidence, UKMLA success, and a processed registration application — and is now either starting their first UK clinical post or has been in post for a matter of weeks.
