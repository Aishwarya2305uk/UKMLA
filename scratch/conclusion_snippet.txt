 once your window opens matters so much.</p>

<h2>Conclusion</h2>
<p>The mechanics of the <strong>UKMLA exam booking process</strong> are straightforward once you understand which system applies to you — medical-school coordination for UK students' AKT sittings, GMC Online through to Pearson VUE for IMG AKT bookings, and a direct GMC-run process for the CPSA — but the margin for costly mistakes is real if you leave booking, adjustment requests, or rescheduling decisions until the last minute. Confirm your eligibility first, watch for booking windows to open, book conservatively with a sensible buffer around other commitments, and submit any reasonable-adjustments request well ahead of booking a slot.
